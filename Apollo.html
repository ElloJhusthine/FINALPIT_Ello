Απόλλων

Apollo

Apollo is the son of Zeus, the king of the gods, and Leto, a Titaness.

Apollo's powers are varied, but he is mostly associated with the power of prophecy. He bestows the gift of prophecy on some of his lovers and favorites. Apollo also has the power to heal, shapeshift, and eternal youth. Apollo's great knowledge and musical skills are also associated with his god-like powers.

Son of Zeus and Leto, and twin brother of Artemis, he possessed many attributes and functions, and possibly after Zeus was the most influential and revered god of all those of classical antiquity.

The Greek God of Music & Light

He made men aware of their sins and was the agent of their ritual purification; he presided over the laws of religion and the constitutions of cities, was the symbol of prophetic and artistic inspiration, and was the patron of the most famous oracle of antiquity, the Oracle of Delphi, and leader of the muses.

He was feared by the other gods and only his father and mother could contain him. He was the god of sudden death, of plagues and diseases, but also the god of healing and protection against evil forces. In addition he was the god of Beauty, Perfection, Harmony, Balance and Reason, the initiator of the young into the adult world, he was connected to Nature, herbs and flocks, and was the protector of shepherds, sailors and archers.

Attributes and Domains

God of the Sun and Light:

Apollo is often associated with the sun and light. In later mythology, he was identified with Helios, the personification of the sun.

Music and Arts:

Apollo is the god of music, arts, poetry, and the leader of the Muses. He is often depicted with a lyre, a stringed musical instrument.

Prophecy and Knowledge:

He is the god of prophecy and oracles. The Oracle of Delphi, one of the most important oracular sites in ancient Greece, was dedicated to him.

Healing and Medicine:

Apollo has the power to bring both health and disease. His son, Asclepius, is a god of medicine.

Archery and Hunting:

Like his sister Artemis, Apollo is skilled in archery. He is often shown carrying a bow and arrows.

Truth and Rationality:

Apollo represents truth, rationality, and order, in contrast to the more chaotic and emotional aspects of existence.
